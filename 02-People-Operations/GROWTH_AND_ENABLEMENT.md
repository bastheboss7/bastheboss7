**Ritual:** A rotating monthly technical showcase.
* **The Rule:** Every team member (including offshore partners) leads one session per quarter.

## üîÑ 3. "Mob-Testing" & Peer Reviews
**Ritual:** Weekly 1-hour "Mob-Programming" session for complex automation logic.
* **The Goal:** The whole team solves one "unsolvable" flaky test or architectural bottleneck together.
* **Impact:** Radical transparency and rapid skill transfer.

---

## üìö 4. The External Voice (Community & Branding)
We encourage the team to represent the organization externally to build personal and company brand.
* **Blogging:** Quarter-yearly technical blog post on the company engineering blog.
* **Open Source:** Encouragement to contribute back to tools we use (e.g., Playwright, Appium).
* **Conferences:** Budget allocated for "Speaker Training" for those accepted to industry events (EuroSTAR, TestBash, etc.).

*Goal: Moving away from "Annual Reviews" to real-time growth.*

3. **The Block:** What process or tool is slowing you down the most?
