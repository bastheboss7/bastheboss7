I use a "Hands-on & Mind-on" approach to hiring SDETs:
1.  **The Mind (Process):** Can they explain the [RBT Matrix](../01-Strategic-Governance/RBT_PRIORITY_MATRIX.md)? Do they understand "Shift-Left"?

3.  **The Heart (Culture):** Do they align with our [Team Working Agreement](./TEAM_WORKING_AGREEMENT.md)?
